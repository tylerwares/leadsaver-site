Will texts come from my business number?

Texts are sent from a dedicated business number powered by proven technologies. This keeps delivery reliable, professional, and compliant—while your existing phone setup stays the same.

Do I have to switch phone providers?

No. LeadSaver works alongside your current provider/number. No changes to your existing phones.

Is this compliant?

Yes. Every message includes opt-out instructions (“Reply STOP to opt out”). We also apply respectful frequency limits (one follow-up per missed-call window).

After hours and weekends?

Yes. LeadSaver responds automatically 24/7 so you can capture more jobs even when you’re busy or closed.

What if I already use other marketing software?

LeadSaver is purpose-built for one job: turning missed calls into booked jobs. It’s simple to add and doesn’t require a new CRM.

Can I set quiet hours?

Yes. You control when messages are sent.
